About Us

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Bharathidasan Institute of Technology started at 2007 offers undergraduate (B.E., Electronics and Communication Engineering) and postgraduate (M.E., Communication Systems Part Time) programmes providing excellent career opportunities in the wide area of Electronics and Communication Engineering.


The Department has a team of faculty members with excellent academic and research experience.

The Department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratory facilities to aid academic and research experiments.
